# SALT and AMT Policy Calculator Grant

**Foundation**: Laura and John Arnold Foundation
**Grant ID**: 24-11708
**Amount**: $94,800
**Duration**: 6 months (July 2024 - January 2025)
**Status**: Completed
**Fiscal Sponsor**: PSL Foundation

## Overview

This grant supported the development of a publicly-available state and local tax (SALT) and alternative minimum tax (AMT) policy calculator, showing how various post-Tax Cuts and Jobs Act (TCJA) reform options affect individuals and the United States.

## Key Deliverables

### 1. Model Enhancements (October 15, 2024)
- **TAXSIM Validation**: Achieved 99%+ accuracy against the gold-standard tax calculator
- **Property Tax Imputation**: Implemented algorithms using American Community Survey data for all 50 states
- **Extended Microdata Forecasts**: Projected through 2034 aligned with Congressional Budget Office
- **Technical Memo**: Documented all improvements

### 2. SALT/AMT Calculator (December 1, 2024)
- Custom calculator integrated into PolicyEngine.org
- Models TCJA extension scenarios
- SALT deduction cap modification analysis
- AMT parameter change analysis
- Household-level impacts
- Distributional analysis
- Federal revenue estimates

### 3. Technical Report (January 15, 2025)
- Comprehensive analysis of SALT and AMT interactions
- Modeling approaches for behavioral responses
- Capital gains realization timing analysis
- Policy guidance for TCJA extension options

### 4. Engagement
- Quarterly check-ins with Arnold Ventures Public Finance staff (September 2024, January 2025)
- Early access and testing for AV staff
