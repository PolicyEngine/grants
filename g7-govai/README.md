# G7 GovAI Grand Challenge

## Overview

The G7 GovAI Grand Challenge is an international competition under Canada's 2025 G7 Presidency, seeking AI solutions for citizen-centric public services.

- **Foundation**: Government of Canada / Treasury Board Secretariat
- **Program**: Rapid Solution Labs
- **Prize**: Up to $10,000 CAD per selected proposal (dedicated 18-29 age category pool)
- **Deadline**: December 1, 2025 at 3:00 PM ET

## Eligibility

- Legal residents of G7 countries (Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, UK, US) and EU
- Teams up to 4 individuals
- Primary contact must be 18+ (or have guardian submit)

## Why PolicyEngine Fits

PolicyEngine directly addresses the challenge's goals of "citizen-centric public services":

1. **Transparency**: Makes complex tax/benefit policies understandable to citizens
2. **Accessibility**: Free, open-source platform anyone can use
3. **Cross-Country Applicability**: Already deployed in US and UK
4. **Practical & Scalable**: Production system serving real users
5. **AI Integration**: Uses microsimulation with potential for LLM enhancement

## Strategic Value

- Visibility with G7 governments and AI policy community
- "Stamp of approval" for government-focused work
- Networking with public sector innovators
- Low effort given existing platform demonstrates the solution
